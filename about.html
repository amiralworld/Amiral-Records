ABOUT
AMIRAL RECORDS is an independent record label built on raw energy, dark aesthetics, and artists who refuse to fit into clean, predefined boxes. We focus on substance over polish, identity over trends, and longevity over noise. Our approach is quiet, deliberate, and uncompromising — we let the work speak for itself. We provide worldwide distribution across all major digital platforms, ensuring that every release reaches a global audience with precision and consistency. Beyond distribution, we actively protect our artists and their work, managing rights, metadata, and release integrity to maintain long-term control and sustainability. AMIRAL RECORDS operates as more than a label — it is a protective framework for artists with a clear vision. We respect creative autonomy and work closely with each project from production to post-release, without diluting its identity. Our priority is to strengthen the artist’s voice, not reshape it for convenience. In addition, we offer PR and promotional services tailored to each release, focusing on visibility, positioning, and impact. Through strategic digital promotion and media outreach, we ensure that the music reaches the right audience, not just a larger one. AMIRAL RECORDS moves with intent. We build quietly, release with weight, and stand behind every project we represent. This is a long-term structure for artists who value control, direction, and authenticity.
